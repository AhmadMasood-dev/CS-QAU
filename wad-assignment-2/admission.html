Admissions

Admission in the department for various degree programmes are offered in Spring and Fall semesters.

Every Semester

PhD (Computer Science)

PhD is the highest degree awarded in an academic discipline. In our PhD programme, students work closely with a faculty member, performing original research, and tackling challenging and unsolved problems.

The minimum requirements for admission are:

  • MPhil (Computer Science or computing related discipline)
  • MS (18 years Education) with Computer Science or computing related discipline
  • Must pass the test and interview arranged by the department

Fall Semester

MPhil (Computer Science)

The objective of M.Phil Computer Science program is to train students to initiate research work.

The minimum requirements for admission are:

  • M.Sc. (Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Technology)
  • 4 years BS (CS, SE, IT)
  • Must pass test (sample test available) and interview arranged by the department

Every Semester

MS (Information Science & Technology)

The aim is to design and develop information applications using IT by understanding the relationship between digital information, people, and technology.

The minimum requirements for admission are:

  • 16 years of education: BS (4 years) or MSc in computing-related disciplines
  • Experience in digital information management
  • Must pass test and interview arranged by the department
Click here to see more details about this programme
Every Semester

MS (Data Science)

Students are exposed to programming, data analysis, visualization, and big data analytics. It prepares them for careers and further PhD in data science.

The minimum requirements for admission are:

  • BS or MSc (16 years) in computing disciplines
  • OR BS/MSc (non-computing) with 2 math + 1 stats courses in final degree
  • Must pass non-credit deficiency courses (if needed) and departmental test/interview

Fall Semester

BS (Computer Science)

Trains graduates to design, implement, and manage systems with computational, communication, and multimedia capabilities.

The minimum requirements for admission are:

  • Intermediate with Mathematics
  • FSc pre-medical with Additional Mathematics
  • Or equivalent 12-year education with Mathematics
