Academics

The department offers PhD, MPhil, MS (IST), M.Sc. and BS degrees
Each degree programme has a specific objective and focus.
The course of study and syllabi for each degree is updated and is inline its objectives.

PhD

PhD is the highest degree awarded in an academic discipline and is the most satisfying and rewarding educational experience. Students work closely with a faculty member, performing original research, and tackling challenging and unsolved problems.

See more

MPhil

The objective of M.Phil Computer Science programme is to train students to initiate research work. The duration of M.Phil programme is two years (4 semesters). MPhil is of 50 credit hours, 24 credit hours for the course work and 26 credit hours for research work. Each course is of 3 credit hours.

See more

MS

In today's Information Society, expertise in all forms of information is required for progress in science, business, and education. The main aim of MS programme in Information Science & Technology (IST) is to design and develop information related applications using information technology by understanding relationship between digital information, people and technology. The MS programme intends to bring together Computer Science and collaborative environment of how people and society interact with digital information.

See more

MS-DS

Data Science has become important due to the need for analyzing and understanding ever-increasing data generated by a multitude of sources. Students will be exposed to programming, data structures, algorithms, data analysis, visualization, and big data analytics, preparing them for international markets or a PhD.

See more

BS

The BS Computer Science program at QAU provides theoretical and practical knowledge for software development in different fields. Students learn system design, implementation, and management. Specializations include Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Platform Development, Human-Centered Computing, Networks and Security, and Information Management.
